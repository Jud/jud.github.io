Hey 👋, I'm Judson Stephenson. Most people call me Jud.

We live in an amazing time. So much to build and so many tokens to burn.

Most recently I was a Staff Engineer at Block working on Bitkey. Before that I ran the ad platform at Drizly (acquired by Uber) and a product consultancy helping startups find market fit.
